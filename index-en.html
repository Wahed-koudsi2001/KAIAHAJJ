Prohibited Baggage:

1. Rounded and irregular baggage.

2. Bags wrapped with clothes.

3. Bags tied with ropes.

4. Bags with long straps.

5. Clothe-made baggage.

6. Baggage weight exceeding the requirements of your airline.

×

Purchasing and Shipping Zamzam Water:

1. Purchase the allowed number of containers from official sales points at the airport.

2. Only one container is allowed / pilgrim.

3. Ship it in the designated shipping belts.

4. Hajj visa through Nusuk application is required.

5. Do not place the container inside baggage.

×

Zamzam Sales Points:

1. Terminal 1 (Gate C2, Gate B2, Welcome Terminal)

2. North Terminal (next to Gate 4)

3. Hajj and Umrah Terminals (Area B, Area C)

×

Boarding Pass Issuance Tips:

1. Group pilgrims, should arrive no less than 4 hours and no more than 6 prior to the flight.

2. Individual pilgrims, should arrive 2 hours prior to domestic flights and prior to international flights.

3. Ensure completing all required travel documents.

4. Verify baggage compliance with the allowed weight onboard.

5. Place irregularly shaped baggage in cardboard boxes.

6. Uneven baggage to be wrapped through available wrapping service at the airport.

7. Have a valid travel ticket to your next destination.

Cash amounts and items that must be disclosed during your travel:

1. Cash amounts SAR +60,000.

2. Jewelry, gemstones, and precious metals worth SAR +60,000.

3. Goods in commercial quantities.

4. Items subject to selective tax.

5. Prohibited and restricted goods and materials.

Automated People Mover (APM) to reqch to the boarding gate in Terminal 1:

Tips for using the APM:

1. Avoid standing near the carriage gates from inside and outside.

2. Avoid blocking the doors of the carriage when closed

3. Avoid sitting in areas not designated for seating

4. Keep the place clean and avoid throwing waste inside the carriages.

5. Do not use the emergency handles except on emergency cases.

6. While standing, hold onto the handle to help you maintain balance and avoid falling.

7. Leave the seats for the elderly, children, and handicapped.
