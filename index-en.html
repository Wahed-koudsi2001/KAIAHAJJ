Tips and Guidance for Departing Travelers

at King Abdulaziz International Airport

Departing Flights

Welcoming terminals strategy for pilgrims

01

Buses to arrive at the site; 6 to 8 hours before the flight departure time.

02

A special lane is allocated for buses entry to the airport.

03

Buses screening point to ensure smooth flow and to verify flight number, destination, departure date, and time.

04

Directing the bus to the allocated area beside the terminal.

05

Passengers wait in air-conditioned halls until the start time of the travel procedures.

06

Directing the campaigns and passengers to the departure terminal.

07

Dropping off passengers in front of the allocated terminal..
